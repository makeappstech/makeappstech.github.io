Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how All HD Video Player ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information in compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD).

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this page.

1. Information We Collect

For a better experience while using our application, we may request certain permissions necessary for the app to function properly. Your personal data is not collected directly by us and your media files remain on your device.

We may collect the following information:

  • Device Information – Device model, Android version, language, and unique device identifiers.
  • App Usage Information – Anonymous usage statistics that help improve app performance.
  • Crash Reports – Diagnostic information used to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Permissions – Storage and media permissions required to access your local videos.

2. Legal Basis for Data Collection (GDPR)

We process data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent provided by the user.
  • Contractual necessity to deliver app functionality.
  • Legitimate interests to improve performance and user experience.

3. Permissions Requested

To provide all features, the application may request the following permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO – Access videos stored on your device (Android 13+).
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Access local media files (Android 12 and below).
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Save or manage files on supported Android versions.
  • MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Manage video files where applicable.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Display playback notifications.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK – Continue playing videos in the background.
  • Picture-in-Picture (PiP) – Allow floating video playback.
  • AD_ID – Used by Google AdMob for personalized advertisements.

4. How We Use Your Information

Information is used for the following purposes:

  • To play videos stored on your device.
  • To organize and manage media files.
  • To provide Picture-in-Picture and background playback.
  • To improve application performance and stability.
  • To display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To fix bugs and improve user experience.

5. Advertising

Our application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, and usage information to provide personalized and non-personalized ads according to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, anonymous usage information may be shared with trusted third-party services such as Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics to improve application quality.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. User Choices

You may deny requested permissions at any time through your device settings. Please note that some features of the application may not function correctly without the required permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that such information has been provided, it will be deleted promptly.
